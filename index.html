The Story

Cooking from the heart,
feeding with intention

I'm a Bangalore-based home cook who turned a lifelong love of western cooking into something bigger — one memorable table at a time.

With over 300 private catering events across Bangalore, I bring restaurant-quality western cuisine directly to homes, terraces, and intimate gatherings. Every spread is made fresh, in my home kitchen, with real ingredients and no shortcuts.

My food is not hotel banquet food. It's the kind of cooking you remember — a slow-roasted casserole that fills the room, a high-tea spread that makes guests feel truly hosted, a BBQ that becomes the story everyone tells.
